OUR MISSION

Fade 2 Karma was founded as a Hearthstone team in June 2015 but since then has developed into something much bigger. Providing gaming and esports entertainment to people all around the globe, Fade 2 Karma has taken its place among the premier esports teams in the CCG genre while also accepting the responsibility that comes with it.

Our broadcasters are role models for hundreds of thousands of people across the globe while providing a fun learning environment. We want to use that influence to present the products of our partners which will make people’s lives happier, easier and healthier. Our mission is to grow both our team and our community and we firmly believe that being positive role models is just good karma.
